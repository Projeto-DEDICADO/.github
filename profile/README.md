The **DEDICADO System** is a research and development initiative focused on the **automatic detection of forest disturbances** using **Earth Observation image time series**.

The project aims to support **near real-time environmental monitoring** over the **Brazilian Legal Amazon (BLA)** and the **Cerrado biome**, combining remote sensing, time series analysis, and artificial intelligence.
